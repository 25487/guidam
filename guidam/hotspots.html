Hotspots

Here you can find a list of spots in Amsterdam to meet other students!

#1 Vondelpark

The Vondelpark is an park in Amsterdam, is one of the most popular hangout spots for students. It is the biggest park in all of the Netherlands and is ideal for a nice and relaxing walk or bike ride!

#2 Café de Gieter

Café de Gieter is a student favorite for a weeknight drink. It’s always energetic, and if you’re trying to meet real students from Amsterdam and abroad, Gieter has plenty of strangers eager to drink with you.

#3 HvA/AUAS Campus

The seven AUAS buildings are accessible to every student: there are meeting squares, restaurants, coffee corners, films and cultural activities, and a common room for study associations. It's located on campus which makes it the ideal hangout spot!

#4 Museum Plein

Museum Plein is the ideal hotspot for students, as it is surrounded by interesting museums, lot's of other students and the occasional festival.

#5 Club NYX

Nyx, in the famous Reguliersdwarsstraat, is quite something. The club is known for its diversity and open-mindedness. Thursdays offer an accessible way to get introduced to this legendary venue.
